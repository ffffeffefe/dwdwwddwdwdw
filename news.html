ELECTRON Proxy Shutdown Confirmed

The Rise of ELECTRON Proxy

ELECTRON Proxy was a widely-used proxy that allowed students to bypass firewall and security to help cure boredom. ELECTRON has rose to be used by over 1,000 people accross the United States. Since then, more and more people have used ELECTRON.

Challenges and Controversies

Despite its success, ELECTRON Proxy faced its fair share of challenges. The service navigated a landscape fraught with legal and ethical considerations. The increasing pressure from copyright holders and the wave of DMCA requests were significant hurdles. These issues culminated in the recent decision to shut down, a move driven by the need to comply with legal requirements.

A Journey Worth Remembering

ELECTRON is a proxy to be remembered by many because of it's many choices of games to play. ELECTRON will be remembered.

The End of an Era

ELECTRON Proxy has no choice but to confirm their shutdown on August 25th, 2024 before any lawsuits were commited. The owner of ELECTRON on GitHub replied to this saying, "We did not want any legal actions so we do not get sued. We have no choice but to shut down. We are closing all websites that use ELECTRON on September 1st, 2024. ELECTRON Proxy although WILL be open-sourced on GitHub for anyone to use. We hope you understand."
